Baseball Scoreboard Premium Instructions:

  • Tap on Game Name input to give your game a name.
  • Tap on Visitor and Home to add your team names.
  • Tap on any inning score to highlight the number and use the + and - buttons to adjust the score for that inning.
  • Tapping on the Hit or Error numbers will increment each.
  • Tapping on Balls, Strikes, and Outs will increment each of them. 4 Balls will reset Strikes. 3 Strikes will reset Balls and add an Out. 3 Outs will reset them all.
